About

Rediscover Sanctuary Cap Cana, the Dominican Republic's most exclusive adults-only all-inclusive resort. You'll be delighted by the resort's $45 million modernization as you explore spectacular suites, artfully crafted lobbies, updated restaurants and bars, enhanced world-class spa and state-of-the-art fitness center.

Sanctuary Cap Cana, A Luxury Collection Adult All-Inclusive Resort is located in Cap Cana, a private gated community retreat inside Punta Cana. Cap Cana is made up of 30,000 acres of flawless beaches, crystal-clear turquoise waters, and lush tropical landscapes that make it one of the most sought-after destinations in the Caribbean. The resort offers an unparalleled all-inclusive experience with personalized butler service, gourmet dining, and world-class spa treatments that will leave you refreshed and rejuvenated.
